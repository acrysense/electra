Flat-Foot Technology

Our patented Flat Foot Technology® is essential. It provides an efficient, relaxed riding position that allows you to sit upright instead of bending over the handlebars like on traditional bikes. This technology allows you to optimize the effort of pedaling with proper leg extension due to our unique frame geometry and lower center of gravity. Simple as that! There are some mechanical engineering details to back this up, but you’ll just have to trust us on that.

Internal Gear System

Internal or i refers to a system that houses all the gears inside a weatherproof rear hub. Internal multi-speed hubs are available in 3, 5 or 8-speeds and have wider gear ratios. Internal gear systems are low-maintenance, easy-to-use and allow you to change gears without pedaling or even standing still. It's not magic, it's engineering.

When is the grand opening?

The Electra Bluewaters Island shop is to be open and ready to welcome customers in the beginning of November 2022. You will be able to try out any bike and choose the best one for your needs and liking. Still if it’s too tempting for you to wait for the official opening, you can fill out the pre-order form now and get your bike before anyone else.

When and how will I get my bicycle?

When the order is placed we will contact you to confirm all the details. You will be able to receive your purchase during 14 days after the confirmation. We can deliver bike to you free of charge, or you can take it by yourself at our store

Do these bikes sell in Dubai only?

The whole world is riding Electra. We have more than 50 shops on almost every continent – except Antarctica, of course. Now the residents of Dubai can enjoy the amazing Electra bikes too.

Where to ride a bike in Dubai?

Dubai has a very developed cycling infrastructure, so you do not have to specifically look for a place to ride your Electra. Moreover, these bicycles have excellent riding characteristics, and you can easily go where other means of transport go. Just climb on and pedal, and you will see the city in a whole new way.

Where do I get my bike maintenance from?

We love and value our customers, and we can easily fix any problem at our salon. But, we are sure that Electra will not give you any trouble, because we pay special attention to the selection of components and assembly of each bike.
